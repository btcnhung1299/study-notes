**PDF**:
$$\mathrm{beta}(x; \alpha, \beta) = \mathrm{c} \cdot x^{\alpha - 1} (1-x)^{\beta - 1}$$
where $c$ is a constant, $\alpha, \beta > 0$ is shape parameters.

*Observations*:
- When $\alpha = \beta = 1$, we got the PDF of uniform distribution: $\mathrm{beta}(x; 1, 1) = \mathrm{c}$
